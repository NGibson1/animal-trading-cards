Lion

A lion's roar can be heard 8 Kilometers away.

  • Scientific Name: Pantheea Leo
  • Average Weight: Males- 420 Lbs, Females- 280
  • Average Lifespan: Males- 8-10 years, Females 15-16 years
  • Habitat: Open plains to thick brush and dry thorn forest.

The Lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera native to Africa and India
